LISTEN TO THE STORY

Bernardo, a tiny boy of just four, gets home and says, in a
way that almost no one but his mother understands:

“The teacher told us to do a research project on the
human body.”

She asks if he knows what that is.

“I dooooo, mom. It’s an arm, then another arm,
a leg, then another leg, a foot, another foot…’

“A head, then another head.” The father jokes.

“Nooooo, Dad, just one head.”
